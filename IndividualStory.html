• Day 1

    Intro to Books That Grow

    When Jessica reached out to the English Language Learning community in her new neighborhood of Queens, NY, they refered her to a new and innovative producat called Books That Grow. It allowed Jessica to read at a level that was easier for her to comprehend and she was hooked from the first leveled sentence.

  • Day 14

    Gaining Momentum

    Jessica had started reading books at Level 2 and 3 but after just two weeks she started to revisit those books and challenege herself with higher levers like 4, 5, and occasionally even 6. Using Books That Grow's define and pronounce features she was able to begin to learn new words she hadn't known before.

  • Day 30

    Using New Knowlege

    By day 30, Jessica was able to gain another level in most of Books That Grows texts and also start really understanding the meaning of the texts she was reading. She started to use new vocabulary words learned in books in conversation and found herself becoming a better writer as well as becoming a strong, independent reader.

  • Day 90

    The Prize

    After a full three months of using Books That Grow, Jessica was able to pass the TOEFL and gain entry to the University she had wanted so badly to go to. Jessica also learned valuable insights on American culture and history through various texts she was able to read with the product.
